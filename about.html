Anupa is a skilled Digital Communicator with a diploma in Media Communications (2022) and a degree in Digital Communications (2024) from Humber College, now pursuing a diploma in Computer Programming at the institute.

Media Communications

During Anupa's time in the Media Communications program, she developed her skills in photography, cinematography, web design (UI/UX), graphic and motion design, strategic writing, marketing and advertising and content creation. Although the majority of her time was spent learning online due to COVID, she developed a stronger connection with her love for photography and UI/UX. It has prompted her to delve into her admiration for wildlife photography. She has since developed a strong interest in photography, UI/UX, graphic design, and writing due to its boundless creativity.

The transition from online schooling to in-person schooling, and the transfer from practical learning to theoretical learning, was a drastic and difficult change for Anupa. April - September 2024

After 15+ job rejections and having no alternative plan in mind for after completing her degree, Anupa applied to enroll in the Computer Programming program at Humber to further her web skills and languages. She was quickly accepted to the program and chose to study online for its flexibility. This worked out in her favor, as in July, she recieved the opportunity to assist and manage a not-for-profit organization's social media. Initially worried about how she would manage full-time schooling and freelance working, she has been astonished at how she has juggled schooling, working, and being there for her family after spending 4 years jumping between Brampton, Etobicoke, Toronto, and Ajax.
