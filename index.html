About me

My name is Anupa Ragoonanan. I am a digital communicator - graduate of Humber's Media Communications (2022) and Bachelor of Digital Communications (2024) programs. Ever since I was small, I loved photography and visual arts. This factored into a lot of my education and professional choices. I now do freelance photography, graphic design, web design & development, and content writing for individuals. My main goal is to develop my photography professionally while also working a full-time job in the web design industry.
